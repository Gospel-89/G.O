About me

Gospel Ononwi is tech savvy who is passionate about the growth of tech in his country (Nigeria) and Africa as a whole.
He is the winner of the AR/VR African 2020, 2nd place winner of blackathon, he is the founder of ExtendAfrica.
He has trained more than +5000 people in several tech skills online and on-ground in Nigeria, the Bath Spa University Academic Center Ras al Khaimah UAE, Coding Dojo in Seattle, and Saudi Digital Academy(SDA) in Saudi Arabia on ARVR technologies and others

He was awarded the most influential AR/VR developer of the year 2022 at the techgrammy award organized by Harvoxx Techhub.
